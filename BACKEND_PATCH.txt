# PATCH INSTRUCTIONS

To enable CORS + health endpoint in FastAPI (`src/app.py`):

1. At the top of `src/app.py`, add:
   from fastapi.middleware.cors import CORSMiddleware

2. After `app = FastAPI(...)`, add:
   app.add_middleware(
       CORSMiddleware,
       allow_origins=["*"],
       allow_credentials=True,
       allow_methods=["*"],
       allow_headers=["*"],
   )

3. At the bottom of `src/app.py`, add:
   @app.get("/health")
   def health():
       return {"ok": True}
